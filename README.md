# 🌾 Agri Sathoi – AI-Powered Smart Agriculture Platform

## 🚀 Overview

Agri Sathoi is an AI-driven platform designed to help farmers make smarter decisions about crop planning, selling, and market engagement.

It combines market access, predictive intelligence, and essential agricultural services into a single, easy-to-use application built for real-world agricultural needs.

---

## ❗ Problem

Farmers face major challenges such as:

* Unfair crop pricing and middlemen dependency
* Lack of reliable and verified bulk buyers
* Limited awareness of real-time market trends
* Difficulty in deciding when to sell or what to grow
* Poor access to relevant government schemes

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Agri Sathoi provides a unified platform that enables farmers to:

* Sell crops through verified agencies
* Compare buyers based on price, location, and trust score
* Access AI-based crop price predictions
* Receive smart crop recommendations based on weather
* Explore farming equipment and resources
* Easily discover and access government schemes

---

## 🧠 AI Decision Engine

The core intelligence of the platform that provides:

* Best time to sell crops
* Price trend insights (increase / decrease)
* Clear recommendations (Sell Now / Wait)
* Risk analysis based on weather and demand fluctuations
